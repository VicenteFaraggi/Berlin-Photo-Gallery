Görlitzer Bahnhof

Since the 1990s, Görlitzer Park has been located on the site of the old Görlitz train station , into which some remnants of the train station have been integrated.

Landwehr Canal

A canal that goes across the whole district of Kreuzberg and Neukölln. During summer people go with their little boats and enjoy the sun floating.

Berghain

The most famous and iconic Berlin techno club, situated between Kreuzberg and Friedrichshain is famous for their 48 hours non stop partying during weekends.

Plänterwald

The territory was part of the Berlin district of Treptow until 2001 and was created as an independent district in 1997 as a separation from the districts of Alt-Treptow and Baumschulenweg.

Street Markets

The city is recognized for his street markets. These usually work 2 days per week and its the perfect panorama for enjoying the day.From veggies and fruit till the most variable handmade crafts you can find here. 

Spätkauf or Late Shops

Famous late shops that works 24/7 in Berlin. Here you can buy your bascis needs and usually offers a huge selection of the best beers. You can also drink theme there and even sometime they offer bathrooms.

Memorial to the Murdered Jews

Holocaust Memorial for short , in the historical center of Berlin commemorates the approximately six million Jews who were murdered under the rule of Adolf Hitler and the National Socialists.
